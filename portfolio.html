Shooting Focus

Core directions where I bring the most value as a DP and editor.

Food & Product

Precision lighting, macro details and motion that makes food and products feel tactile and alive. Focus on clean composition, textures and appetising motion.

Music & Performance

Energetic camera work and edit rhythm tailored to the track. Experience with performance-led clips, low-light setups and stylised lighting.

Documentary & Weddings

Invisible camera, real behaviour and honest storytelling. From weddings to corporate documentaries – capturing emotional beats without directing people.

Tech & Startups

Clean visuals for innovative companies: interviews, founder stories, product explainers and launch films for tech brands.
