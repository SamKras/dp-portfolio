Shooting Focus

Core directions where I bring the most value as a DP and editor.

Food & Product

Precision lighting, macro details and motion that makes food and products feel tactile and alive. Focus on clean composition, textures and appetising motion.

Music & Performance

Energetic camera work and edit rhythm tailored to the track. Experience with performance-led clips, low-light setups and stylised lighting.

Documentary & Weddings

Invisible camera, real behaviour and honest storytelling. From weddings to corporate documentaries – capturing emotional beats without directing people.

Tech & Startups

Clean visuals for innovative companies: interviews, founder stories, product explainers and launch films for tech brands.

Why choose me

A mix of commercial discipline and documentary intuition.

Commercial precision

Over 100+ projects for brands across 15+ countries. I understand agency expectations, brand guidelines and the need to deliver visuals that convert, not just look good.

Documentary sensitivity

Documentary weddings and corporate docu work trained me to be invisible, anticipate emotion and respect real people in front of the camera.

End-to-end thinking

As both DP and editor, I shoot with the cut in mind. This keeps sets efficient and post-production faster, while protecting the final rhythm of the film.

Flexible geography

Based in Belgrade, regularly available in Dubai and across Europe. Comfortable working with local and remote teams, small agile crews or full productions.
